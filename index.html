About the branch

The Bognor Regis & Littlehampton Reform UK branch works to give local residents a clear voice on national and local issues – from border control and defence to council tax, housing and town centre safety.

Mission of the Bognor Regis & Littlehampton Branch

BRAL Mission Statement

Our mission is to build a fairer, freer and more accountable community by empowering local people to have a real voice in how they are governed.

We stand for common-sense reform – restoring trust in politics, revitalising our local economy and protecting the rights and responsibilities of everyone.
