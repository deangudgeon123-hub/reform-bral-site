Reform UK wants young members

Our Bognor Regis & Littlehampton branch is building a team of young members to help shape our digital presence and bring fresh energy to local campaigning.

Young Members & Youth Engagement

Reform UK wants young members – and so do we

If you’re 25 or under and care about what’s happening in the country, we want you on our side. Join our Social Media & Youth Engagement team and help us reach younger voters across Bognor Regis & Littlehampton.

Join or Donate Email the branch

Who we’re looking for

We’re looking for young people who use social media every day and understand how people your age actually talk online. You don’t need political experience – just an interest in what’s happening in the country and a willingness to get involved.

  • 25 and under, living in or around Bognor Regis & Littlehampton
  • Care about issues like cost of living, housing, crime and the future of the UK
  • Want experience you can put on a CV, portfolio or UCAS application
  • Happy to share honest feedback on what works with younger voters – and what doesn’t

What you can get involved in

As a young member, you can help with:

  • Social media content – help create and share posts, stories and short videos
  • Youth outreach – help us connect with younger voters online and at local events
  • Campaign days – join street stalls, leafleting and public meetings when you can
  • Ideas & feedback – tell us what lands with people your age – and what doesn’t

You can give as much or as little time as you’re able. We’ll always work around college, work and family commitments.

What you’ll get out of it

By volunteering with our branch you can gain real campaign and communications experience, build a portfolio, and be part of a team driving national momentum.

  • Real campaigning and communications experience
  • Content and projects you can show in a portfolio
  • Skills in social media, public speaking and teamwork
  • Meet like-minded people who care about the future of the country
  • A strong foundation if you ever want to stand as a candidate or take on a bigger role in politics

You never know – the experience you gain here could be the first step towards becoming a future political leader.

Ready to get involved?

If you’re 25 or under and want to help, we’d love to hear from you. Email us with your name, age, postcode and how you’d like to help (social media, events, design, video, etc.).
